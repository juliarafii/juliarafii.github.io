Introduction

Wander

Wander's coffee shop offers unique coffees in an effort to give people wandering by the ocean the jump start they need to a productive, feel-good day.

Young, trendy coffee drinkers of San Francisco

Coffee drinkers who put their heart and soul into finding coffee made with intention, balance, and a love for the endless surf of the California coast. Wander visitors celebrate what it means to sit back and enjoy a cup of coffee along with appreciating a special eye for design.

Wander works to deliver a diverse mix of hand shaped surfboards, beautiful crafts, both new and vintage clothing, rad records, and cosmic coffees. The items carried are of the highest quality and created in an ethical manner, encouraging a high-vibrational lifestyle.
